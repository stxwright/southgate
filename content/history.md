Established in 1882, **Southgate Cycling Club** has the distinction of being amongst the very earliest cycling clubs in the world. We are based in Enfield, North London, and welcome riders of all abilities.

## Our Heritage

Since our founding in the late 19th century, the club has been a cornerstone of the North London cycling community. We have weathered two world wars and seen the evolution of cycling from high-wheelers to modern carbon machines.

## Tradition and Community

Today, we continue to uphold the traditions of our founders while embracing modern cycling culture. Our mission is to promote cycling for all abilities and ages, maintaining the friendly and inclusive atmosphere that has defined us for over 140 years.
